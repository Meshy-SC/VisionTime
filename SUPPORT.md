# VisionTime Support

**Welcome to VisionTime Support.** We are here to help you get the most out of your entertainment tracking experience.

## Supported Platforms

VisionTime is available on the following Apple platforms:

- **iOS** — iPhone
- **iPadOS** — iPad
- **visionOS** — Apple Vision Pro
- **macOS** — Mac
- **tvOS** — Apple TV

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Getting Started

**How do I add a movie or TV show to my library?**
Use the Search tab to find a title, then tap the "+" button to add it to your library.

**How do I track my watch progress?**
Open a title from your library and mark episodes as watched, or mark a movie as watched from its detail page.

### iCloud Sync

**Does VisionTime sync across my devices?**
Yes! If iCloud is enabled, your library, watch progress, ratings, and notes sync automatically across all your Apple devices signed in to the same iCloud account.

**How do I enable or disable iCloud sync?**
Go to **Settings > [your name] > iCloud** on your device and toggle VisionTime on or off.

**My data is not syncing. What should I do?**
1. Ensure you are signed in to iCloud on all devices.
2. Check that iCloud sync is enabled for VisionTime in your device settings.
3. Make sure you have an active internet connection.
4. Try restarting the app.

### Data and Privacy

**What data does VisionTime collect?**
VisionTime only stores the content you track (movies, shows, watch progress, ratings, and notes) and your app preferences. We do not collect personal information, location data, or analytics. For full details, see our [Privacy Policy](PRIVACY_POLICY.md).

**How do I delete my data?**
- **Local data:** Delete the app from your device.
- **iCloud data:** Disable iCloud sync for VisionTime in your device settings, then delete the app.

### Troubleshooting

**The app is crashing or not responding.**
1. Force-quit the app and reopen it.
2. Ensure your device is running the latest OS version.
3. Update VisionTime to the latest version from the App Store.
4. If the issue persists, contact us (see below).

**Search results are not loading.**
VisionTime uses The Movie Database (TMDb) to fetch content information. Please check your internet connection and try again. If the problem continues, TMDb service may be temporarily unavailable.

**I found incorrect information about a movie or show.**
Content information is provided by TMDb. You can help improve their database at [themoviedb.org](https://www.themoviedb.org/).
